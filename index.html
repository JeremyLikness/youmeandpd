You, Me, and PD is a unique conversation between Jeremy Likness who was diagnosed with young onset Parkinson's Disease in early 2020 and his caregiver and wife, Doreen. They host candid conversations about living with Parkinson's Disease, share stories and tips, and discuss the physical and emotional impact of the diagnosis. Learn about their approach to exercise, nutrition, supplements, faith, estate planning and coping with grief. Join them on a special journey through challenge and triumph and share in their combined hope for a cure on the horizon.
